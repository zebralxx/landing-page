Meet the app

The perfect app to learn to sing

Our Artificial Intelligence algorithm allows us to remove the original voice and keep the melodies of the songs. This way, we have the largest musical repertoire for Karaoke! In addition, when you sing, your voice and pitch are compared by the algorithm allowing an accurate evaluation of your performance, helping you improve your singing.

The largest Karaoke music library!

Our AI magically removes vocals from any song and makes it sing-ready!

Gamified experience

Your performance is scored according to our ultra-precise algorithm. Challenge yourself, gain recognition and celebrate your progress!

Record your performances

Whether you want to share with friends or track your progress, our audio and video recording feature lets you make the most of your experience!

Share with the community

Share your recordings with the community, interact and explore other people's vocal performances in your feed!

Real-time lyrics

Never miss a beat with real-time synced lyrics. You can also turn on the song's original voice to help.
